🔬 Water Science — Know the Truth
What RO filters really do

Reverse osmosis removes 92–99% of calcium and magnesium. The membrane works by diffusion through polymer — only uncharged molecules like H₂O with no hydration shell can pass through.

Minerals like Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺ carry an electric charge. That charge attracts surrounding water molecules forming a hydration shell — making the ion 3–6× larger than it looks. Too large to pass through the membrane.

Result: pure H₂O comes out the other side — nutritionally empty. Brands then add back tiny amounts of cheap mineral salts just enough to print numbers on the label.
